## New framework: "Could a superfan answer this instantly?"
For each character dossier, add a section that answers:
1. **Daily-life facts:** hobbies, food preferences, habits, routine quirks.
2. **Relationship granularity:** like/dislike map with confidence and reason.
3. **Named assets:** vehicles, weapons, aliases/titles/codenames.
4. **Specific encounters:** named animals/NPCs and why they matter.
5. **Behavioral tells:** speech patterns, stress reactions, comfort rituals.
6. **Trauma ledger:** key wounds, source event, behavioral consequence.
7. **Arc triggers:** what changes them, where, and why.
8. **Ending state:** physical, emotional, and symbolic endpoint.
9. **Quote-proof points:** at least 3 scene-backed examples per major claim.
10. **Disputed-fact handling:** mark unresolved details with "open research" tags.

Use this checklist when expanding every dossier revision.
