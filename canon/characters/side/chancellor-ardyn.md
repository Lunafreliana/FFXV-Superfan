# Chancellor Ardyn

## Who am I?
Chancellor Ardyn is a supporting FFXV character with a distinct role in the broader journey ecosystem.

## What do I look like? 
A flamboyant, polished political figure whose styling signals unpredictability and control.

## What is my story?
He appears courteous on the surface while quietly manipulating timing, trust, and route pressure around the protagonists.

## What has shaped me?
Long-burn grievance structures, strategic patience, and elite political maneuvering.

## What is my psychological profile?
Manipulative, highly observant, and emotionally performative.

## What is my story arc from beginning to end?
Begins as ambiguous helper and evolves toward explicit destabilizing antagonist function.

## What are my relationships like?
With Noctis side: proximity-through-deception dynamic. With imperial system: instrumental partnership.

## What do I think of...[character]?
He judges others by how effectively they can be steered, provoked, or broken.

## What are my fighting or other skills?
Psychological pressure, misdirection, and narrative-sequencing control.

## What are my hobbies?
Provocative conversation and social gamesmanship are recurrent behavior patterns.

## How do I speak?
Polite, ironic, and loaded with hidden intent.

## How do I act?
Creates uncertainty, then exploits responses.
