# Canon Factions: Civic and Commercial Organizations
## Analysis
### Hunters and Meldacio HQ
Hunters function as quasi-civil defense infrastructure across the road regions where state military capacity is uneven. Meldacio HQ appears as an organizing node with long-memory ties to pre-collapse Lucian authority networks, indicating that hunter institutions outlast formal regime breaks and become part of continuity governance.
### Lucian service-economy organizations
Canon material about major enterprises (Kenny Crow, Leville, power utilities, roadside motels) shows that factions in FFXV are not only military or royal. These organizations mediate everyday life under crisis: mobility, food, lodging, power, and information. Their persistence under war pressure demonstrates social resilience layers beneath headline geopolitics.
### Causal role in narrative flow
These groups are structurally important because they keep routes viable and communities functional while imperial, daemon, and Astral shocks escalate. Without them, the party’s movement and survivor logistics would collapse.
