# Canon Locations: Key Sites
## Insomnia
- **Location context:** Capital of Lucis and seat of the Crystal during the modern era.
- **Event(s) tied to site:** Niflheim invasion; theft of the Crystal; death of King Regis; final return in late game.
- **Canon constraints/details:** The Wall/royal barrier historically protects Insomnia via Crystal + Ring power.
- **Source note:** Main game chapter framing; chronology notes in project source-intake logs.
## Tenebrae / Fenestala Manor
- **Location context:** Oracle lineage territory tied to House Fleuret.
- **Event(s) tied to site:** Noctis is sent there for healing in childhood; later occupied/conquered by Niflheim (except Fleuret manor zone in some records).
- **Canon constraints/details:** Oracle tradition continuity is anchored to this region.
- **Source note:** Character chronology summaries and Eos history notes.
## Altissia
- **Location context:** Major Accordo city-state setting and a central political/religious turning-point location.
- **Event(s) tied to site:** Leviathan covenant sequence and major party/trajectory rupture.
- **Canon constraints/details:** Frequent hinge point in canon-divergence analysis due heavy narrative consequences.
- **Source note:** Main game progression and cross-media discussion.
## Angelgard
- **Location context:** Isolated island associated with Noctis's post-Crystal reemergence.
- **Event(s) tied to site:** Talcott reconnects with Noctis after the ten-year gap and routes him back to survivors.
- **Canon constraints/details:** Marks transition from Crystal stasis to World of Ruin return.
- **Source note:** Late-game character timeline notes.
## Hammerhead
- **Location context:** Key Lucian outpost/garage hub tied to Regalia operations.
- **Event(s) tied to site:** Regalia maintenance and survivor regrouping in post-timeskip context.
- **Canon constraints/details:** Functions as practical continuity anchor across early and late arcs.
- **Source note:** Main-game travel structure and supporting-cast context.
## Royal Tomb network (Lucis)
- **Location context:** Distributed tomb sites containing Royal Arms and kingly legacy markers.
- **Event(s) tied to site:** Noctis's Royal Arm collection and succession burden progression.
- **Canon constraints/details:** Tied to Lucian royal lineage and chosen-king preparation.
- **Source note:** Main game arc + lore references.
