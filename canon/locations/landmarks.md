# Canon Locations: Landmarks
## Analysis
### Disc of Cauthess
The Disc of Cauthess is one of the most important geographic landmarks in the overland arc: it is linked to Titan/Archaean awakening, imperial blockade activity, and a major narrative escalation from regional travel to Astral confrontation.
### Fenestala Manor
Fenestala Manor is a landmark of political trauma and historical pivot. Canon chronology ties it to the attack that killed Sylva and deepened Tenebrae’s occupation context.
### The Citadel (Insomnia)
The Citadel anchors Lucian court authority and Crownsguard duty context. It appears as both ceremonial/political seat and contested wartime symbol.
### Taelpar Crag
Taelpar Crag is treated as a myth-linked rift associated in legend with the Great War of Old, giving it landmark importance beyond simple geography.
### Angelgard (Umbral Isle)
Angelgard is a visually distinct offshore landmark repeatedly called out in travel/photo contexts and later tied to Noctis’s reemergence stage.
### Rock of Ravatogh
Ravatogh is a volcanic landmark zone in Cleigne, tied to rare flora references and major traversal/trial framing in route-level geography.
### Ghorovas Rift (Shiva battle/frozen site context)
Ghorovas Rift is the explicitly named location where Shiva (the Glacian) awakened and was killed by the imperial army in chronology material, making it the key landmark for the “frozen Shiva battlefield” historical reference.
### Royal Tomb network (landmark complex)
The Royal Tombs are a distributed landmark system tied to the Armiger legacy and kingly duty progression rather than a single site.
### Zegnautus Keep
Zegnautus Keep is a late-arc imperial landmark associated with research archives, security systems, and high-risk confrontation spaces.
