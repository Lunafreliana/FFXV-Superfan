# Canon Locations: Regions and Cities
## Analysis
### Insomnia
Insomnia remains the political and symbolic core of Lucis in the modern era: Crown City, Crystal seat, and Wall-centered final bastion. Its status explains why treaty-day assault on the capital equals regime collapse rather than a routine battlefield loss.
### Leide
Leide is the early-route Lucian region where everyday mobility, roadside economy, and post-collapse uncertainty are most visible. It grounds the transition from royal departure to lived wartime geography.
### Duscae
Duscae functions as the mid-arc pressure region where Astral-scale events, imperial activity, and infrastructure stress are experienced in close succession. It is a key bridge from road-trip cadence to crisis escalation.
### Cleigne
Cleigne is both industrially active and conflict-exposed, serving as a western corridor for late overland progression and as the home-region context for Lestallum and Old Lestallum continuity.
### Tenebrae
Tenebrae is a sacred-political territory: homeland of the Oracle line and long-term occupied zone under imperial pressure. It is central to both Noctis/Lunafreya backstory and the broader Lucis–Niflheim struggle.
### Accordo / Altissia
Altissia is the principal urban node of Accordo in the main arc. It is simultaneously diplomatic stage, covenant theater, and narrative rupture point where personal and civilizational stakes converge.
### Lestallum
Lestallum is the primary modern city hub in Cleigne and a recurring logistics anchor for survivors, commerce, engineering capacity, and chapter transitions.
### Old Lestallum
Old Lestallum preserves regional memory and civic continuity, explicitly linked in signage/plaque material to the development of modern Lestallum and later supervision by Meldacio Hunter HQ.
### Cape Caem (Caem)
Cape Caem functions as a strategic hideaway and transit staging node (including Regalia insignia references and sea-route preparation context). It marks a strong transition space between overland and maritime arc phases.
### Galdin Quay
Galdin Quay is a coastal transit city-node where Lucis-to-Altissia movement, delay politics, and public-facing war news intersect early in the journey.
### Cartanica
Cartanica is a late-route rail station node that marks overland contraction and increasing reliance on constrained transit corridors.
### Gralea
Gralea is the imperial capital-space and late-arc destination theater connected to Crystal recovery, infection/quarantine documentation, and endgame escalation.
### Meldacio HQ
Meldacio HQ is the institutional hub of the hunter network and an important place-level anchor for civil defense continuity beyond formal military structures.
### Solheim (ancient civilization domain)
Solheim is an ancient civilizational geography referenced in modern political and technological history; its legacy is explicitly tied to Niflheim’s imperial ambitions and war capacity development.
