# Canon Locations: Travel and War Theaters

## Analysis

### Galdin Quay and maritime transition
Galdin and the sea-route staging sequence mark the pivot from overland Lucis traversal to Altissia-bound political/religious escalation. This transition is repeatedly treated as phase-changing in both narrative and game-structure language.

### Cartanica and train corridor
Rail segments and stations (including Cartanica context) represent late-route compression where imperial pressure and daemon risk converge in transit spaces. These locations are less about settlement identity and more about constrained movement under crisis.

### Gralea / Zegnautus axis
Imperial capital-adjacent spaces and installations function as culmination theaters for antagonistic state power, magitek doctrine, and late-stage revelations. They are core to the shift from regional survival to decisive endgame confrontation.
