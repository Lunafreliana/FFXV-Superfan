# Canon Lore: Items, Relics, and Signature Artifacts

## Crystal
- Divine focal artifact of Lucis-prophecy structure.
- Tied to chosenness, Ring power transfer, and final purification providence.
- Strategic target in the Insomnia invasion.

## Ring of the Lucii
- Royal conduit between king and Crystal.
- Enables high-cost sacred/forbidden power use tied to Lucian line.
- Endgame fulfillment requires full light transfer and blood-price sacrifice.

## Royal Arms
- Legacy weapons of past Lucian rulers.
- Mechanically empower Noctis; narratively reinforce inherited burden.
- Sourced through royal tomb network.

## Trident of the Oracle
- Sacred weapon/gift tied to Bahamut's blessing of the first Oracle line.
- Symbol of Oracle authority and covenant duty.

## Regalia
- Royal car used by Noctis and companions across Lucis.
- Symbolically carries Noctis-Regis relationship weight in developer/story framing.
- Operationally central to travel, pacing, side-quest cadence, and survivor reconnection loops.

## Old Wall / Twelve royal statues
- Defensive/ritual structures tied to kings of old.
- Framed as caskets/residences of prior kings' souls in historical-lore descriptions.
- Associated with Insomnia's defense mythos.
