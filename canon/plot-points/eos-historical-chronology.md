# Canon Plot Points: Eos Historical Chronology (Concise)

## Mythic/Ancient frame
- Solheim rises with advanced machine/magitek civilization.
- Ifrit turns against humanity and conflict escalates into ancient astral war context.
- Early king-oracle structure forms (Lucis Caelum line + Fleuret oracle line).

## Starscourge emergence era
- Starscourge spreads and causes mass death and daemonization.
- Ardyn (as Ardyn Lucis Caelum) is celebrated as healer by absorbing scourge into himself.
- He is later rejected as impure, and succession history is rewritten through Somnus-led victory framing.

## Kingdom-era consolidation
- Lucis is established around Crystal custodianship and Ring inheritance.
- Oracle rites and Astral slumber/waking cycle become embedded in prophecy system.
- Royal king legacy structures (tombs/arms/wall traditions) formalize succession burden.

## Modern-era geopolitics
- Lucis, Tenebrae, Accordo, and Niflheim align to shared modern calendar baseline.
- Niflheim expansion policy leads to conquest campaigns and imperial militarization.
- Barrier-era standoff forms after Lucis uses Crystal/Ring power to shield Insomnia.

## Late-modern pregame and game-era events
- Ardyn enters Niflheim political-scientific apparatus in modern era and rises to chancellor role.
- Noctis departs on treaty-marriage route; Insomnia falls shortly thereafter.
- Main game arc proceeds through covenants, Royal Arms, and final kingly providence.
