# Canon Plot Points: World-State Escalation

## Analysis

### Escalation chain
The world-state deterioration arc proceeds from territorial war pressure to infrastructural fragility, then to night expansion and daemon dominance. This chain explains why late-game stakes exceed regime restoration and become civilizational survival.

### Imperial research as multiplier
Magitek and daemon research logs provide an explicit mechanism for escalation, showing policy-level decisions that weaponize scourge-adjacent phenomena and accelerate breakdown.

### World of Ruin framing
The ten-year interval is not merely a time skip; it is a documentation of unmitigated trend continuation while the chosen resolution remains pending.
