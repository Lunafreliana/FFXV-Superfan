# Master Canon Timeline
## Analysis
This timeline should be read as a dependency chain, not isolated dates. The early mythic settlement (Crystal/Ring/Oracle duties) establishes legitimacy rules; imperial expansion converts those rules into territorial conflict; Wall-era contraction redefines Lucian strategy; treaty theater triggers regime collapse; and the final decade-gap arc resolves the Starscourge crisis through sacrificial kingship. The key continuity principle is that political, religious, and cosmological timelines are the same timeline viewed at different scales.
