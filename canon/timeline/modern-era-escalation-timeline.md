# Modern-Era Escalation Timeline
## Analysis
This supplement narrows the master timeline to late modern causality: imperial militarization, treaty deception, state collapse, and post-collapse dark-era continuation. It is intended as a quick operational chronology for readers tracking war-to-apocalypse transition.
