# Story: Mermaiding

This folder is for **mermaiding story-specific** material.

- Keep canon facts in `canon/`.
- Keep reusable fanon patterns in `fanon/`.
- Keep project-only choices, adaptations, and planning here.

## Reference catalog
- `reference/reference-catalog.md` — index of what exists and what still needs to be filled in.
- `reference/characters/` — story-specific character profiles, relationships, voice notes.
- `reference/locations/` — story-specific place usage, geography notes, sensory details.
- `reference/plot-points/` — story beat planning, reveals, and foreshadowing hooks.
- `reference/artifacts/` — story-use objects, symbols, and custom constraints.
- `reference/timeline/` — story chronology and continuity tracking.
